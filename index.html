How NeuroMap Works

System architecture, philosophy, and operational constraints.

1. Origin & Philosophy

NeuroMap was developed as a targeted intervention for the Neurofields Discord server. We observed a recurring pathology: newcomers paralyzed by infinite choices, and veterans repeating the same methodological advice regarding dataset confounding. This hub replaces ephemeral chat advice with immutable, queryable infrastructure.

Curation over aggregation. Link dumps are anti-patterns. Every item in the Pathfinder is vetted for actual portfolio utility. If an entry does not result in a demonstrable artifact (a repository, a paper, a verifiable skill), it is omitted.

2. Preflight AI Architecture

The Analysis Preflight leverages open-weights models (via OpenRouter) strictly as a methodological adversary. It is prompted to reject vague assumptions and output strict JSON. It acts as an automated Peer Reviewer 2 before you begin coding.

Limitations: The AI does not know the exact idiosyncratic directory structures of specific datasets (like an un-BIDS-ified lab dataset). It will hallucinate statistical tests if your question is overly broad. Treat the checklist as a heuristic filter, not ground truth.

3. Graph Mechanics

The Career Graph utilizes D3.js force-directed scaling (`d3.forceSimulation`). It operates on four node strata: Backgrounds, Decisions, Subfields, and Outcomes. Transition speeds are capped at 150ms to enforce tool-like responsiveness.
